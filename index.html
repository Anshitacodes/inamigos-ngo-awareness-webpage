WHO WE ARE

Creating Positive Change Across India

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla and is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization based in Chhattisgarh.

The foundation is 80G & 12A certified, CSR-1 registered, NITI Aayog registered and ISO 9001:2015 certified.

Through a strong network of volunteers, interns, professionals and community partners, the organization works towards creating sustainable social impact across India.
